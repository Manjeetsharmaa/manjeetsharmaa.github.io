HTML5/CSS3

HTML and CSS: A strong understanding of HTML (Hypertext Markup Language) and CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) is essential for creating the structure and styling of a website.

Responsive design

Responsive design: The ability to create websites that are optimized for viewing on multiple devices, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones, is crucial in today's digital landscape.

Graphic-Design

Graphic design: A good eye for design, color, typography, and layout is important for creating aesthetically pleasing websites.

User experience (UX)

User experience (UX) design: The ability to create websites that are intuitive, user-friendly, and accessible is critical for ensuring a positive experience for visitors.

JavaScript and front-end frameworks

JavaScript and front-end frameworks: JavaScript is a programming language that allows for dynamic interactions on websites, such as form validation, image sliders, and pop-ups. Familiarity with front-end frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue can also be valuable.

Content management systems (CMS)

Content management systems (CMS): Experience with CMS platforms like WordPress, Drupal, or Magento can be useful for creating and managing websites that require regular updates.

Search Engine optimization (SEO)

Search engine optimization (SEO): Knowledge of SEO best practices can help improve a website's visibility in search engine results pages and attract more organic traffic.
